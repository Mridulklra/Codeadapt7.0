Rounds

Code Adept consists of 3 rounds with an intent to boost your confidence and upgrade your skills. These rounds are thoughtfully kept in CodeAdept aiming to prepare you for placements. Here are the details about various Rounds -

01

Aptitude Round

Aptitude Based Round consists of Aptitude Questions to test the analytic thinking of the candidate. There will be Technical Questions too which will not be any language specific.

02

General coding

Brainstorming and Logical Round consists of Easy Coding and Debugging Problems to test the candidate's logical reasoning. This round requires brainstorming ideas to reach the optimal solution.

03

Core Coding

Hard Level Coding Round consists of High-Level Coding and Debugging Questions. This round will test the problem-solving ability of the candidate. It will boost up your confidence.
